Why a Climate Information System?

Climate change is reshaping our planet. A climate information system brings together data, context, and guidance so anyone can understand what is happening and what can be done.

Monitor

Track vital signs of the Earth, including temperature anomalies, atmospheric CO₂, and sea level rise.

  • Historical and live indicators
  • Global and regional views
  • Interactive visualizations

Understand

Turn complex scientific data into clear narratives and visual stories that are easy to grasp.

  • Explanations of key concepts
  • Context behind the numbers
  • Science-backed summaries

Act

Discover practical steps for individuals, communities, and governments to reduce emissions and adapt.

  • Evidence-based actions
  • Impact estimations
  • Tracking of commitments

From Insight to Action

Every fraction of a degree matters. Explore how choices at different levels can shift our trajectory.

Individual

Reduce your own footprint and inspire others through daily choices.

  • Switch to renewable electricity where possible
  • Prioritize public transport, cycling, or walking
  • Cut food waste and choose climate-friendly diets

Community

Work with local groups, schools, and organizations to transform systems around you.

  • Support community solar or energy projects
  • Advocate for greener city planning
  • Participate in local climate adaptation plans

Policy & Industry

Encourage policies and innovations that drive large-scale emission cuts and resilience.

  • Back science-based climate targets
  • Invest in clean technologies
  • Align finances with a net-zero future
